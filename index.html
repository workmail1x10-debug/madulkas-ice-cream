Our Story

Our Journey

15 years in the making.

From a small lakeside stand to a Worcester summer staple — here's the story of Madulka's.

2011
🍨

The Beginning

Madulka's opens its doors on Lake Avenue with a simple idea — bring great ice cream to one of the most beautiful spots in Worcester. A small walk-up stand with big ambitions and even bigger views.

Early Years
🍫

Hand-Crafted From Day One

Every scoop is handmade with care. We built our menu around classic flavors done right — premium vanilla, rich chocolate, house butter pecan — and slowly grew to 32+ flavors that keep regulars coming back.

The Ritual
🌞

A Worcester Summer Tradition

Before long, we weren't just a shop — we were part of the season. Families showed up after dinner, couples came for the sunset, and the lakefront buzzed every weekend. The dogs, the kids, the sunset — that's the Madulka's feeling.

2021
🎉

A Decade on the Lake

Ten years in, we looked back and realized we'd become a landmark. The lake, the pink sign, the line at the window on a summer night — it all became part of the Worcester summer.

4.5 ★
💘

Loved by the Neighborhood

720+ verified Google reviews and a 4.5-star rating later, we know we're doing something right. But more than the numbers, it's the kids asking to come back every day, the parents remembering their own childhood trips here — that's what keeps us going.

2026
🍩

15 Years Strong

Fifteen seasons. Thousands of sundaes. Countless sunsets on Lake Quinsigamond. We're excited to welcome you back for another unforgettable spring and summer — bring the family, bring the dog, bring your appetite.

Why Apply

Why you'll love working here.

Working at Madulka's isn't just a paycheck — it's the summer you'll look back on for years.

🌞

Best "Office" in Worcester

Work on the lake. Catch the sunset every shift. Fresh air, water views, and the kind of vibe you can't get anywhere else.

💕

A Close-Knit Crew

We keep the team small and tight. You'll know everyone by name, make real friends, and become part of a Madulka's family.

💰

Great Tips, Happy Customers

Our regulars are loyal and generous. Serve a great scoop with a smile and the tips take care of themselves.

📅

Flexible Scheduling

Perfect for high-school and college students. We work with your schedule, your exams, your life. Spring and summer only.

📚

Real-World Skills

Customer service, teamwork, handling busy rushes, money handling — skills that stay with you long after summer ends.

🏞

Part of a Worcester Tradition

The Role

What to expect.

🍦

What You'll Do

  • Scoop & serve with care — hand-crafted cones, sundaes, and specialty treats
  • Greet customers with energy — you're the face of Madulka's
  • Take orders at the window, ring up sales, handle tips
  • Keep the stand clean, stocked, and ready for the next rush
  • Pitch in on opening & closing duties each shift
  • Help out the team — we work together, always
⭐

What We Look For

  • 16 years old or older — required for all positions
  • Friendly, upbeat, and genuinely enjoys people
  • Reliable — shows up on time, every time
  • Comfortable on your feet during busy summer rushes
  • A team player who takes pride in the little things
  • Available weekends, evenings, and sunset shifts
